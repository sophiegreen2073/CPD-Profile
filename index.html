About Me

My name is Sophie Green, I am a 24 years old and studying Msc Robotics at the University of Manchester. I have a Bsc in Maths and Computer Science also from the University of Manchester that I obtained in 2022. I have spent three years working as a Software Developer, focusing on warehouse automation and simulation at a manufacturing company..

Skills

  • Programming in Python, C#, HTML, Javascript, AngularJS, MySQL and ROS.
  • I have experience using Emulate3D for simulation.
  • Writing MES solutions that handshake with PLCs for controlling elements of automation within warehouses (including cobots, vision systems etc.).

    I would love to continue working in software, but move towards a more robotics focused career. I am particularly interested in medical robotics as they are on the cutting edge of innovation and are helping improve health all around the world.

    Projects

    These are a few projects I've done at both my job and at University.

    Automated Labelling Line

    I created a MES system for an automated labelling line, labelling full bags of pet food. This was a bespoke solution, incorporating Universal Robots, cameras with build in deep learning models and PLCs for controlling the individual components.

    Manufacturing and Distribution Warehouse Simulation

    I am working on an ongoing simulation of an automated packing hall for packing dry pet food into bags. This includes simulating the new version of the distribution warehouse. I used Emulate3D, reading CAD designs and outputting throughput numbers to support the design process.

    Leo Rover Object Retrieval

    In a dynamic team as part of my master's degree we have been building a Leo Rover with a manipulator arm to retrieve coloured blocks and place them in a bin of the same colour. This requires using ROS and RViz to control the Rover and Manipulator arm.
